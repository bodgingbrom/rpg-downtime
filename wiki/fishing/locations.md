# Locations

Five fishing spots, gated by skill level. Each has its own fish roster, theme, and base cast time.

| Location | Level Req | Base Cast Time | Difficulty |
|----------|-----------|----------------|------------|
| Calm Pond | Lv1 (default) | 15 min | 🟢 Easy |
| River Rapids | Lv2 | 25 min | 🟡 Medium |
| Deep Lake | Lv3 | 35 min | 🔴 Hard |
| Misty Grove | Lv4 | 45 min | 🟣 Arcane |
| Cloudmere | Lv5 | 55 min | ⚫ Apex |

Use `/fish locations` in-game to see which are unlocked for you, plus trophy status at each.

## Legendaries Only Exist in Active Mode

Legendary catches never appear during AFK sessions. They only roll when you're actively fishing. See [Active Mode](active-mode.md) for how the legendary encounter actually plays out — it's a conversation with a unique character, not just a bigger fish.

Each location has **one** specific legendary at any time — a persistent named character with their own personality and memory of past encounters. Catching them retires them; a fresh character is generated to take their place.

## Unlock Timing

Approximate XP to reach each unlock:

| From → To | Approximate Time (AFK, basic gear) | Active Mode (faster) |
|-----------|-----------------------------------|---------------------|
| Lv1 → Lv2 (100 XP) | 1-2 hours of AFK | ~20 min active |
| Lv2 → Lv3 (300 XP) | 3-5 hours at River Rapids | ~1 hour active |
| Lv3 → Lv4 (600 XP) | 5-8 hours at Deep Lake | ~2 hours active |
| Lv4 → Lv5 (1000 XP) | 6-10 hours at Misty Grove | ~2.5 hours active |

Fishing XP comes from catches: trash = 1, common = 5, uncommon = 15, rare = 40, legendary = 100, all scaled by the location's skill level. The Human race gets a +15% XP multiplier globally.
