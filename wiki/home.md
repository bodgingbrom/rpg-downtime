# Downtime Bot Wiki

Welcome to the Downtime Bot! This bot provides mini-games to play between D&D sessions, giving your group something fun to do during downtime.

## Available Games

### [Downtime Derby](derby/index.md)

A complex racing game where you own, train, breed, and race fantasy creatures. Place bets on daily races, compete in weekly tournaments, and build a championship-winning stable.

**Quick Links:**
- [Getting Started](derby/getting-started.md) - Buy your first racer and learn the basics
- [Your Stable](derby/stable.md) - Managing, training, and caring for your racers
- [Racing & Betting](derby/racing.md) - How races work and how to place bets
- [Breeding](derby/breeding.md) - Produce foals with inherited stats
- [Tournaments](derby/tournaments.md) - Weekly ranked competitions with big prizes
- [Admin Guide](derby/admin.md) - Server setup and configuration

### [Lazy Lures](fishing/index.md)

A fishing mini-game with two modes. AFK fishing lets you commit bait and walk away for steady passive income. Active fishing is weirder — bites come every 30-90 seconds and each one is a little moment (a whispered secret, a one-word vibe check, a haiku you finish, or a conversation with a fish that remembers you).

**Quick Links:**
- [Getting Started](fishing/getting-started.md) - Your first cast, bait basics, earning coins
- [Gear & Bait](fishing/gear.md) - All four rod tiers, all four bait types, cast speed math
- [Locations](fishing/locations.md) - Calm Pond, River Rapids, Deep Lake and their fish
- [Progression](fishing/progression.md) - XP, levels, fish log, location trophies
- [Active Mode](fishing/active-mode.md) - Whispers, vibe checks, haikus, legendary encounters
